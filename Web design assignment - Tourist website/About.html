About Canberra Tourism

Canberra Tourism was created back in 1985 under the name Canberra Tourist Advisory.
It was a rocky start, with many people that we approached for sponsorships, turning us away.
But, we braved through, and eventually struck a merger deal with Tourism Canberra in 1998.
Since then we have changed our name to Canberra Tourism. We are also proud to announce that
as of 2015, we have gotten our own mascot named David the Dog. Who has brought in many customers
Currently we cover about 76% of Canberra. But, we hope to make that 80% by the end of the year.

As for what we do. We do what we love most. Helping the people of Canberra have a good time.
If you want to find a certain place in Canberra, we have it. As Steve Jobs once said, "We have
an app for that", here at Canberra Tourism our saying is, "We have a place for that." Whether
it is entertainment, recreation or relaxation. We know where you need to go, and we can even
tell you the events going on there as well. So that you don't need to research. In the future
we are looking to add phone alerts, so if you are a member, look out for it in the future.
