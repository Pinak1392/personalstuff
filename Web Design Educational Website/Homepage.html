About Us

Fake Image

At Space Maths, we strive to teach the children of Australia maths. We offer courses that range from Kindergarten to year 8. We also offer online Tutors that you can talk to for help. From 2016, we have also started competitions in order to reward our children for their intelligence. Last year we had a reward where the winners got to come down to Canberra to meet us.
