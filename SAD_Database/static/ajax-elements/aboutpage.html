Crew Designations

Helm Control

Controls the ship and gives sensor designations to all members and personnel present. A good helmsman is the difference between parts of your ship being compromised, and a successful mission against all odds. Values speed and efficiency.

Operations

Handles all elements of the ship requiring finesse and precision. Assists the Command staff with ship duties, handles power and alternative operations, keeps things precise and allows the crew to have a sense of understanding of the mission at all times.

Command

Has oversight over the whole team. Collaborates the team effectively and leads in dangerous scenarios. Assisted by all team members to ensure a successful operation through their commanding presence. They will lead our ship into the glorious frontier and establish peaceful or less-than-peaceful relations in their wake.

Science

To establish a bold frontier, first we must understand them. We will build our way through the stars on paved roads of our understanding of them. They research elements of the ship and control power generation onboard the ship, as well as shields in a firefight.

Tactics & Weapons

All of history has shown us conflict's inevitability. To this end, our tactics control user will prevent a catastrophe onboard the ship by eliminating all possible threats. They will control the weapons and how to use them, alongside feeding the information on what they are doing to the Captain.
